\begin{figure}[htbp]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=\textwidth]{figures/fig_mode_switcher.pdf}
\caption{Mode-switcher analysis: composition vs.\ compensation. Four panels compare ``switchers'' (37,024 users who used TUB pre-ban and rode STD/ECO in December) with ``never-TUB'' riders (79,668 users). (a) December 2023 STD/ECO mean speed distributions. (b) Pre-ban (October--November) STD/ECO speed distributions showing the pre-existing gap ($d = 0.24$, small). (c) Within-user speed change distributions; the user-level DiD is $+0.20$~km/h ($d = 0.069$, negligible). (d) Effect size summary across metrics, confirming that all differences are negligible to small. The analysis rules out risk compensation: switchers do not ride faster in STD/ECO mode after losing TUB access.}
\label{fig:mode_switcher}
\end{figure}

(b) Comparison of placebo and treatment DiD effects, confirming that the December speed change is attributable to the TUB ban rather than differential seasonal trends between user groups.}
