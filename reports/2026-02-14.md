### Mixed-Effects Model (Task 5.3)

**MixedLM (N=100K, REML):**
- ICC (user random intercept) = 0.271 -- 27% of speed variance is between-user
- Key fixed effects on mean speed (km/h):
  - TUB mode: +2.20 km/h (p<0.001)
  - ECO mode: -2.56 km/h (p<0.001)
  - Age 30-34: -0.79 km/h (p<0.001)
  - Service road: +0.65 km/h (p<0.001)
  - Footway: -0.27 km/h (p<0.001)
  - frac_major_road: -2.22 km/h (p<0.001) -- congestion effect
  - log(distance): +1.50 km/h (p<0.001)
  - Weekend (Sat): -0.77 km/h, (Sun): -0.69 km/h (p<0.001)
  - Evening: -0.65 km/h vs midday (p<0.001)

**OLS Full Dataset (N=2.31M):**
- R-squared: 0.2815
