## Issues / Blockers
- **OSM maxspeed coverage**: Very low in Korea (1-12%). This limits Task 3.4 (extracting speed limit attributes from OSM). May need to assign default speed limits by road class instead.
- **Full GLMM infeasible**: statsmodels BinomialBayesMixedGLM too slow for 2.3M obs / 329K groups. Used standard logit + GEE as complementary approaches. Could consider R's lme4 or Julia's MixedModels for a proper GLMM if needed.
- **GMM K selection**: BIC does not plateau with N=198K. K=4 selected for interpretability but this needs discussion in the paper.
- **ECO mode OR > 1**: ECO has slightly higher speeding odds than STD (OR=1.17), which is counterintuitive. This may be because ECO users are a self-selected population that also frequently uses TUB mode. Need to investigate with interaction terms or within-user modeling.
