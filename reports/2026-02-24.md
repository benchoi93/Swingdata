### New results integrated:
1. **Experience effect**: Speeding 3.8% (1st trip) -> 28.0% (500+ trips); GEE OR=1.178 per doubling; mediated by TUB mode adoption (11.5% -> 47.8% over 50 trips)
2. **Distance-speeding**: Monotonic 13.9% -> 54.2% across deciles; spline pseudo-R2=0.387
3. **Road composition ORs**: Service 2.74, tertiary 2.05, major composite 6.74; primary/secondary protective
4. **Speed drift**: Riders slow down -1.5 km/h in second half
5. **Curvature paradox**: Higher curvature -> lower speeding (OR=0.716) but 5x higher risk when speeding occurs
6. **SHAP**: TUB mode SHAP=2.33 (3.6x next feature); LightGBM AUC=0.905

### New policy recommendation:
- **Graduated mode access**: Restrict new riders to ECO/STD modes for initial trips; unlock TUB after demonstrating safe riding
