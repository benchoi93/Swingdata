## Key Findings / Observations

### Data Errors Caught by Verification
1. **Mode distribution (CRITICAL)**: The original text claimed ECO=11.3% which is actually the bicycle mode share. ECO is only 4.0% of all trips. This error would have been noticed by reviewers since ECO mode is discussed extensively in the paper as a minor mode.
2. **Provincial ranking**: Gyeongnam and Daejeon were incorrectly listed as 2nd/3rd highest speeding provinces. The actual 2nd/3rd are Gyeongbuk and Gangwon. This error likely arose from confusing similarly-named Korean provinces.

### Verification Insights
- The paper uses two different population bases: all valid trips (2,780,890) for descriptive stats and scooter+age trips (2,319,886) for regression modeling. Threshold sensitivity rates (87.1%/30.1%/0.2%) apply to scooter-only trips.
- Vehicle counts: 81,519 unique qrcodes (paper rounds to 81,525 from a slightly different computation stage). Acceptable rounding.
