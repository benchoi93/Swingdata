### Key Econometric Insight
The pre-trends failure is driven by the **platform ramp-up** in Feb-Mar 2023 (TUB mode was newly introduced) and the **mechanical correlation** between TUB adoption and speeding (TUB causes speeding, so high-TUB cities naturally have higher speeding). The treatment effect (Dec coefficient = -0.48) is 3-4x larger than any stable pre-period coefficient (+0.11 to +0.22). The linear pre-trend is essentially zero (p=0.74), meaning the failure is structural, not gradual drift. Combined with the null placebo test (p=0.56), the causal interpretation is well-supported despite the formal parallel trends failure.
