## Key Findings / Observations
- **Cumulative fabrication rate: 21/49 entries (42.8%) had some form of error.** Phase 10.1 found 10 entries with completely wrong metadata (fabricated papers, wrong journals). Phase 10.2 found 11 more with wrong author names, missing authors, or wrong venues.
- **Author name fabrication is systematic**: First names were consistently hallucinated while last names were correct. All 10 first names in degele2018 were wrong. All 5 in badeau2019 were wrong. This pattern suggests the LLM had access to citation-level metadata (author surnames) but fabricated given names.
- **Venue fabrication pattern**: Fabricated venues were always in the same field and plausible (e.g., Foot & Ankle Specialist instead of Bone & Joint Open; TRR instead of Sustainability). The fabrication was sophisticated enough to include volume/number/pages that looked real.
- **Two entries remain unverifiable** (chen2016hotspot, chen2017bicycle). For chen2016hotspot, the claimed TRR volume 2587 pages 86-93 are occupied by different papers (Blanc et al. pages 78-89 and Dill & McNeil pages 90-99). For chen2017bicycle, neither Crossref nor Semantic Scholar has a matching paper by Chen, Shen, Childress with this title. Both should be checked by PI before submission.
- **PI's own paper (jin2022transformer) was missing co-author Jiwon Kim.** This is a striking illustration of how bibliography errors can be overlooked when they involve familiar citations.
