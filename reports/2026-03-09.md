## Key Findings

1. **Selective causal response**: Governance reduces harsh events (Bonferroni sig) but NOT speed_cv, cruise, zero_speed, trip count, or active users
2. **Zero compensation**: Anti-Peltzman on all margins (max |d| = 0.133)
3. **Composition vs compensation**: STD/ECO increases after TUB ban are entirely pool composition, not behavioral adaptation
4. **TUB mediation**: 60-72% of experience-safety gradient is via mode selection, not inherent aggressiveness
5. **No demand cost**: Trip count and active users unaffected by governance
