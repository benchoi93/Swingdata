\item Predict-then-validate framework: cross-sectional behavioral profiles generate testable predictions validated by natural experiment
\item Six predictions about governance effects on safety, demand, and dynamics margins---all six confirmed causally
\item Multi-outcome difference-in-differences with Bonferroni correction identifies selective response: safety margins respond, demand and dynamics do not
\item Within-user compensation test on all unconstrained margins yields zero behavioral compensation ($|d| < 0.14$)
\item Cross-sectional profiling shown to forecast causal policy outcomes---a template for pre-implementation assessment
